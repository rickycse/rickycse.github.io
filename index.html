Q. Do you have any work experience?

Software Engineer Intern at MyRide901

Markham, ON - 3 Months
This was my first internship and I can't even describe how lucky I was. After 500+ applications (some to the U.S), I thought it was time to give up once June crept in, but then I got an email from the co-op program saying a small startup was looking for interns. Machine Learning Intern at TD Bank

Toronto, ON - 4 Months
Lady luck smiled on me once again and I somehow landed an internship at TD Bank for Winter 2023. On paper, I was a "Quality Engineer Intern" but oddly enough, I spent the entire 4 months building and testing machine learning models. I ended up learning a lot about machine learning, a little bit about AI/ML industry, and even got to present my work to a bunch of different teams to have it adopted across the department. I also met a lot of really cool people along the way (hey Isaac, sup Pravin). Overall, I really enjoyed my time at TD and I felt like it took me a solid step forward in becoming a good engineer.

Software Engineer Intern at RBC

Toronto, ON - 12 Months
My most unexpected surprise was getting an internship at RBC for Fall 2024. I applied to some positions thinking, "there's no way RBC would hire me." Well, an OA and short interview later, I got the position! I was really excited to work at RBC because of all the good things I kept hearing about the mentorship, culture, and work. It was a little daunting to hear that I'd be staying for a full year, but I would say that its been well worth it. Getting to see how software engineers work day-to-day fully confirmed for me that this was what I wanted to do in the future. Working for 12-months has also been great so far since its allowed me to really invest myself into a big project and "own it" in a way. The amount of support and mentorship I've received has been honestly insane and I feel like I've learned so much in such a short time. If I could go back in time and could pick my first co-op, RBC would be my first choice.

Little tidbit: One of my highlights so far was accidentally becoming a co-op representative for RBC at UoftHacks lol.
